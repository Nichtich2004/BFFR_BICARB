01 — The Problem

The GI Problem Has
Held Bicarb Back
For 40 Years.

"Sodium bicarb works — but athletes can't use it. It didn't work very well because there was a high percentage of people that just couldn't even line up for the race because it just tore apart your stomach."
Steve Magness — Elite Running Coach & Author

Two-thirds of 2024 Paris Olympic running medalists used bicarb — but this edge has remained exclusive to elites who can afford risky protocols. The hydrogel improved it. It hasn't solved it.

02 — Our Solution

No Stomach.
No Waiting.
No GI Risk.

BFFR
BICARB
Buccal-Delivery Snus-Style Pouch
1
Place the Pouch

Athlete tucks a small pouch under the upper or lower lip — similar to a snus or nicotine pouch — 45–60 min before exercise.

2
Buccal Absorption

Bicarb dissolves and absorbs directly through the highly vascularized buccal mucosa into systemic circulation — no GI tract involved.

3
Buffer & Perform

Blood bicarbonate rises, buffering hydrogen ions produced during intense exercise. Athletes push harder, longer, without stomach risk.

Nicotine pouches (Zyn, On!, Velo) have already proven consumer acceptance of buccal pouches. Athletes know this format — we give it a performance purpose.
